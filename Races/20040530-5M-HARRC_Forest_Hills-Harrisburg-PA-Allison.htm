HARRC Sunday Club Run
May 30, 2004
Race Director:  Al Mercik

 

Forest Hills-Centennial Acres Run

 A cool comfortable morning greeted the 19 members who gathered for the May 30, 2004 club run. Several courses were laid out so runners could choose their level of competition with hills and without hills.  In the main race a 5.175 (GPS measured) Alison Small and Jim Collins led the way. Several members ran an extra loop on the return trip and wound up running 6.05 miles. Frank Leiter led this group.
