DC Road Runners Club Custis 12K
Bluemont Park, Arlington VA, Saturday, February 5, 2005

On a beautiful winter's day, with snow all around, more than 160 runners basked in sunny temperatures into the low 60's at DC Road Runners Club's Custis 12K (7.45 miles) in Bluemont Park on Saturday, February 5.

Despite a dozen patches of ice, James Bales, 25, of Alexandria piled on the sub-6-minute miles to win in 42:33, just 30 seconds ahead of Arlington's Mike Fox-Boyd, 25, second in 43:03.

Running pretty in pink, Arlington's Christine Eckstaedt, 22, caught one guy after another, finally finishing 26th overall and first among women in 52:10.

So slippery was the trail in places that one runner even wore spikes.

Many volunteers helped out, including staff from Capital Hospice, the race beneficiary.
