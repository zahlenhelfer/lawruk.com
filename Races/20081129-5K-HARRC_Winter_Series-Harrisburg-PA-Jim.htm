HARRC Winter Series
Race #1
Riverfront Park
November 29, 2008

Read about DVS of Cumberland & Perry Counties by clicking the logo above.

Race Director: Marge Lebo
Timing:  Walt Greene
Course Set-up/Sweep:  Steve Whittle
Assisted by:  Jennifer Ney,  Charles Laverty, Dave Lebo, Josh Lebo, Steve Whittle, Debbie Whittle

  

Within 48 hours of completing various area “Turkey Trots”, 39 of South Central PA’s finest athletes donned their running shoes for Race #1 of the Winter Series.  Lynn Danner took top honors in 18:39.   Taking third overall was Jennifer Gerhart in 21 flat.

 Congratulations to the Manning family on their first 5K race!  Thirteen-year-old Sean Manning ran a speedy 21:36 and captured the 5th spot overall.  Alex Manning ran a steady 31:35.

 Many of today’s runners and helpers completed marathons this month including: Frank Crupi (New York), Hap Miller (Harrisburg), Dave Lebo (Harrisburg) , Steve Whittle (Harrisburg) and Richard Lengler (Harrisburg).  Most outstanding marathon performance goes to 83-year-old (always-there-when-you-need-him) Charles Laverty, who completed the Harrisburg Marathon this year.

Thanks to all runners for their generosity.  We’re off to a good start with our DVS donation.  Hope to see you all back on 12/20/08!
