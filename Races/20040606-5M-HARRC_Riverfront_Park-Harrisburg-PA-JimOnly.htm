HARRC Sunday Club Run
June 6, 2004
Race Directors: Mike Garman, Walt Greene, Pat Spagnolo, and Randy Murphy

 

30th Year Club Run Anniversary

HARRC celebrated 30 continuous years of club runs by offering a runner’s choice 2 or 5 mile run at the place where it all started.  More than 65 runners, club members, and friends of HARRC converged on Riverfront Park at 8:30 a.m. to commemorate 30 years of club runs and to keep the tradition alive.  Past Presidents Walt Greene, Nick Marshall, and Rich Krampe offered several stories of the inaugural run on June 9, 1974.  Nick Marshall started the race with the original starting bell used by the Harrisburg Mayor to start the inaugural run 30 years earlier. 

Post race activities included refreshments provided by HARRC and a special presentation recognizing club member Jim Honchar’s completion of 50 marathons before reaching the age of 40.  Special thanks was given to Les Powell, a founding member of HARRC, for writing and arranging the printing of the club’s anniversary run in the June 7, 2004 edition of The Harrisburg Patriot News.
