Saint Joseph the Worker 4 Miler

May 20, 2006

Great race overall! Fairly challenging up and back course with beautiful houses, scenic views, and little traffic. The priest gave a nice little blessing before the race and reminded us the fortune of running. There were a decent number of spectators along the way. The last 1/4 mile is uphill with the finish in view. The prizes and food afterward were ridiculusly plentiful! For the $16 race day price, this was an A+ race.
