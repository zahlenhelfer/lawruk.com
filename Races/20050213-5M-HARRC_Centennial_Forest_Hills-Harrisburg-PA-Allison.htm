HARRC Sunday Club Run
February 13, 2005

Race Director: Al Mercik

                                                                   

                          Centennial/Forest Hills "5" Miler

Twenty runners gathered on a beautiful central Pennsylvania morning for the Centennial/Forest Hills run. Becky McClintock led all runners on the five mile course followed by Gary Grobman and Steve Remillard. Alison Small was the second lady across the finish line. My neighbor Victor Murcavage ran a respectable 41:15 in his first Sunday run with HARRC. A couple of people missed one turn but everyone made it back to the finish line. I didn't wind up with any extra keys.
