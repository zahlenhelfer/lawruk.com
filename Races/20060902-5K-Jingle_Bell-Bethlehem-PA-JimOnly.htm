Thank you for doing the 2006 Jingle Bell Race. The results were done as best we could with, despite frequent announcements to check your tag, a fair amount of missing info on them anyway. Also, we had about 8 more times than finishers. This happens sometimes when people go back out on the course to run friends in; we did our best to guess-timate which times to remove, based on random race tags recorded. If your time is not correct, use whatever you recorded on your watch or what you saw at the finish. see you in 2007!!
