HARRC Sunday Club Run
June 15, 2008

Race Directors: Mike Garman & Charley Laverty














          Riverfront Park Runner's Choice 2 or 5 Mile Run

Thirty-five runners celebrated Father’s Day by attending a runner’s choice 2 or 5 mile run.  Many new runners to HARRC runs were present today and we hope they return.  Gary Grobman led the 2-mile pack with a time of 13:03.  Marge Lebo led all women at 16:57.   Tadd Morris led the 5-mile group in 30:40 and the first female was Mary Matthews at a fast 37:39.
