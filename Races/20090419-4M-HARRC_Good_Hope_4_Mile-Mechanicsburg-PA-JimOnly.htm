HARRC Sunday Club Run
April 19, 2009

Race Director: Jim Collins



Good Hope 4 Mile Run
 

Good hope run

We got a really nice day for a run.  We had sun, but not a hot day and a light breeze.  Twelve runners came out to participate in a 4 mile run thru the residential area of Good Hope Farms.  We had some good times for this distance.  A couple of the runners came in from out of state to run this run.  First place over all was Josh Shearer with a time of 27:26 followed by Jim Lawruk with a time of 27:29.  Brandy Berwager was the first place female finisher with a time of 30:03.  Below is the results as recorded.
