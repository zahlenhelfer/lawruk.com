Potomac Valley Track Club Christmas Caper 5K and 10K
East Potomac Park, Washington DC
Saturday, December 20, 2008

In the 5K, Rodney Hulstein, all the way from Yukon Territories of Canada, led the race, and Christy Sporidis finished first among women.

David Roche outlasted Eric Makovsky in the 10K, and US Olympic Women's Marathon Team Trials competitor Desireé Ficker won the women's race.

Race director Rolland Elliott presented holiday awards and thanked all his volunteers: James Barr, Craig Chasse, Sheldon Contract, Jim Darr, Sharon Good, Toni Letaw, Jen Stephens, Jay Jacob Wind, and official starter Jim Flanik.
