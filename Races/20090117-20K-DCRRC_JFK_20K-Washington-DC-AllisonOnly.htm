DCRRC JFK 20K - Washington,DC

January, 17 2009

Saturday, 17 January 2009 17:42
Washington DC

Braving sub-freezing weather, runners from as far away as Alaska, California, and New York came for these Inauguration Weekend races.
Matthew Galewski and Gabrielle J. Thibodeau led the 5K, and Kevin D'Amanda and Jeanette Dumbrell won the 20K.
Race director Daryl Knuth thanks all the volunteers and CycleLife for hosting us before and after the races.
According to sources, this event dates back to January 1961, when JFK was inaugurated.
