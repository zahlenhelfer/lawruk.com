Welcome to the Canonization Pilgrimage to Rome Site!

This site is dedicated to Saint Leonie Aviat and the celebration of her canonization. The members of the canonization pilgrimage give special thanks to the Oblates and DeSales University for making this wonderful trip possible. Be sure to visit www.oblatesisters.org for more information about Aviat.

If you have any questions or comments about this site, feel free to email Jim Lawruk (jim@lawruk.com) or Fr. Tom Dailey (Thomas.Dailey@desales.edu). Please send Jim Lawruk any information or pictures you think would enhance this site. The pictures on the site were provided by Fr. Burns and others along with pictures from the Internet. If you have better pictures, scan them or let us scan them. A resolution of 375x500 or greater is ideal.
